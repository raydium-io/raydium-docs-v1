<Info>
  LP rewards come from two sources: **swap fees** (earned on every trade through your pool) and **farm emissions** (token incentives funded by a project or Raydium itself). The mechanics differ between CPMM and CLMM.
</Info>

## Swap fees

Every swap through a Raydium pool pays a fee. Most of that fee (after a small protocol cut) goes to the pool's LPs, proportional to their share of active liquidity.

### CPMM fee accrual

Fees are added directly to the pool's token reserves after each swap. The total reserves grow; the number of LP tokens in circulation does not. This means each LP token is worth a larger fraction of the pool over time.

You do not need to claim anything. When you withdraw, you receive your proportional share of the grown reserves — principal plus all accumulated fees.

### CLMM fee accrual

CLMM tracks fees per-tick and credits them to in-range positions proportionally. The amounts accumulate as a pending balance attached to your position NFT.

CLMM fees **do not auto-compound**. You must click Collect Fees to transfer them to your wallet. Until you collect, they sit idle on the position account.

To compound, collect the fees, optionally swap to rebalance the ratio, then increase the position's liquidity.

## Farm rewards

Farms are incentive programs that run alongside a pool. Projects fund a reward budget for a fixed schedule, and LPs earn token emissions by staking their LP tokens into the farm.

Farm rewards are **separate from swap fees**. You can earn both simultaneously.

### How emissions are distributed

A farm emits a fixed amount of tokens per unit of time across all stakers. Your share is:

```
Your reward rate = (your staked LP tokens / total staked LP tokens) × farm emission rate
```

If the pool has $1M staked and you have $10k staked (1%), you receive 1% of all emissions during that period.

Farm APR falls as more LPs stake. A 200% APR farm at launch typically drops to 40–60% once the word spreads and liquidity piles in.

### Farm staking mechanics

- **To earn:** stake your CPMM LP tokens into the farm. Your LP is now earning both swap fees (reflected in LP token value growth) and farm emissions simultaneously.
- **To harvest:** click Harvest in the portfolio. Rewards transfer to your wallet. Your LP stake does not move.
- **To unstake:** withdraw your LP tokens. Pending rewards are usually settled as part of the unstake transaction.

CLMM positions have their own built-in reward mechanism and do not use Farm v6. Some CLMM pools attach external reward streams to the pool itself; these are collected the same way as CLMM fees.

## Reward timing

Farm rewards accrue per-second but are calculated and claimable on-chain. There is no penalty for claiming late — unclaimed rewards sit on the farm account indefinitely until the schedule ends.

If a reward schedule ends while you have unclaimed rewards, you can still claim them after the end date unless the farm has been explicitly wound down.

## Claim frequency

Every claim is an on-chain transaction with a network fee. Optimal claim frequency depends on:

| Position size | Suggested frequency |
|---|---|
| < $500 | Monthly or less |
| $500 – $5k | Biweekly to monthly |
| $5k+ | Weekly or when material amount has accrued |

There is no meaningful advantage to daily claims on small positions. The transaction fee outweighs the gas-free compounding benefit.

## Auto-compounding

Raydium does not natively auto-compound CLMM fees or farm rewards. Manual compounding steps:

1. Collect CLMM fees (or harvest farm rewards).
2. Check current token ratio.
3. Optionally swap to match the deposit ratio for your position range.
4. Increase liquidity on the position.

Third-party yield optimisers built on Raydium may automate this, but they carry their own smart contract risk.

## Verification after claim

- Confirm the transaction status is **Success**.
- Check that reward or fee token balances increased in your wallet.
- Refresh the portfolio page and confirm pending amounts decreased.
