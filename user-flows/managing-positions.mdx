<Info>
  CPMM positions are fungible LP tokens — simple to track and manage. CLMM positions are NFTs with a specific price range — they require more active attention. Both live on the Portfolio page.
</Info>

## Finding your positions

All open positions are visible at [raydium.io/portfolio](https://raydium.io/portfolio).

- **CPMM positions** show as fungible LP token balances with their underlying token pair and value.
- **CLMM positions** show as position NFTs with their price range, current status (in range / out of range), and accrued fees.
- **Farm positions** show staked LP balances alongside pending reward amounts.

You can also see CLMM position NFTs in any Solana wallet that surfaces NFTs.

## Managing a CPMM position

CPMM positions require minimal ongoing management.

**To add more liquidity:** open the pool page, click Add liquidity, enter an amount, and sign. Your LP token balance increases.

**To remove liquidity (partial):** click Remove liquidity, choose a percentage or LP token amount, review the estimated token return, and sign.

**To remove liquidity (full):** remove 100% of your LP tokens. This closes your position and returns both tokens.

**Fees:** no explicit collection step. Fees compound automatically into the LP token value. You receive them on withdrawal.

## Managing a CLMM position

CLMM positions need monitoring because the price range can go in or out of range.

### Checking range status

In the portfolio, each CLMM position shows:
- **In range** (green): currently earning fees.
- **Out of range** (orange/red): currently earning zero fees. Consider rebalancing.
- **Current price** vs your lower and upper bounds.

### Collecting fees

CLMM fees accumulate as a claimable balance. They do not auto-compound.

1. Open the position from the portfolio.
2. Click **Collect fees** (or **Collect all** to collect fees and rewards together).
3. Review token amounts.
4. Sign the transaction.

Collect when the accumulated fees are worth more than the transaction fee. For most positions, weekly or monthly is sufficient.

### Increasing liquidity

To add more capital to an existing position:

1. Open the position.
2. Click **Increase liquidity**.
3. Enter an amount in either token. The UI computes the required pair ratio based on your range and current price.
4. Sign.

This does not change your range — it only adds depth to the existing position.

### Decreasing liquidity

To partially withdraw:

1. Click **Decrease liquidity**.
2. Choose a percentage or specific amount.
3. Review the estimated token return. Pending fees may be included in the same transaction.
4. Sign.

### Rebalancing (close and reopen)

When price drifts to the edge of your range, your options are:

- **Wait.** If you expect price to revert, doing nothing is valid.
- **Rebalance.** Close the position, swap tokens to the current price ratio, and open a new position centred on the current price.

There is no in-place range adjustment. Rebalancing is always: close → swap → open.

Steps:

1. Click **Close position** (equivalent to decreasing liquidity to zero).
2. Collect any pending fees in the same transaction if prompted.
3. Check your wallet balance — you now hold the two tokens at whatever ratio the position exited at.
4. Optionally swap to rebalance (e.g., you exited with mostly USDC after SOL fell; swap some USDC back to SOL at the new price).
5. Open a new position centred on the current price with your desired range.

### Closing a position

Closing removes all liquidity, collects pending fees and rewards, and burns the NFT. Rent from the NFT account is returned to your wallet.

1. Click **Close position**.
2. Review the token amounts and pending fees.
3. Sign.

After closing, the NFT disappears from your wallet and portfolio.

## Farm positions

If your CPMM LP is staked in a farm:

- **Harvest rewards:** click Harvest on the farm position. Rewards go to your wallet; stake remains.
- **Unstake:** withdraw your LP tokens. This usually harvests pending rewards in the same transaction.
- **Add stake:** deposit more LP tokens into the farm to increase your emissions share.

## Monitoring

Key metrics to watch on the portfolio page:

| Metric | What to do |
|---|---|
| CLMM "out of range" | Consider rebalancing |
| Low pending fees (CLMM) | No action needed; collect when worthwhile |
| Farm reward APR falling | Normal as more LPs stake; check if total return still meets your target |
| One-sided token balance (CLMM out of range) | Price has moved significantly; decide to rebalance or wait |
