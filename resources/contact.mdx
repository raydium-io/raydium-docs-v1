Before asking for help, check the [FAQ](/getting-started/faq) and [Trust & Safety](/getting-started/trust-and-safety) — most common issues are covered there.

<Warning>
  Raydium support will **never** DM you first, ask for your seed phrase, or ask you to "verify" your wallet by connecting it to an external site. If someone claiming to be Raydium support contacts you directly, it is a scam.
</Warning>

## Report a bug or security issue

| Type | Where to report |
|------|----------------|
| **Smart contract vulnerability** | [Immunefi bug bounty](https://immunefi.com/bug-bounty/raydium/information/) — rewards up to $500k |
| **Docs mistake, broken link, stale code** | [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/raydium-io/raydium-docs-v1/issues/new) |
| **App (UI) bug** | Discord **#feedback** channel |

See [Security → Disclosure](/security/disclosure) for the full vulnerability disclosure policy.

## Build on Raydium (integrations & partnerships)

For business development, partnership proposals, and integration enquiries:

- **Discord:** post in **#bd-and-integrations** or DM the BD team after an intro in Discord
- **Email:** bd@raydium.io

When reaching out, briefly describe your project, the integration you have in mind, and any relevant traction or timeline.

For technical integration questions (SDK usage, API, CPI patterns), use Discord **#dev-support** — the BD contact is not a technical support line.

## Token listing

Raydium pools are **permissionless** — any token can be listed without approval by creating a CPMM or CLMM pool. There is no listing fee or process.

- [Create a CPMM pool](/user-flows/create-cpmm-pool) — standard pool creation, takes ~5 minutes.
- [LaunchLab](/user-flows/launchlab-overview) — for new token launches with a bonding curve that graduates to a pool.

For large-scale or co-marketed listings that require Raydium's promotional support, reach out via the BD contact above.
